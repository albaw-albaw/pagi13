Education & capacity building programs

From postgraduate degrees to executive immersions — delivered in Singapore, India, and Southeast Asia.

Postgraduate Program

International Professional Master's Program (iPMP) at NAMTECH

Industry-focused postgraduate program at NAMTECH in collaboration with TUM (Technical University of Munich), preparing engineering graduates for Industry 4.0 roles in Smart Manufacturing, AI, and Sustainability. The curriculum spans four semesters, beginning with a strong foundation and progressing to advanced concepts, a summer internship, and an industry Capstone Project. Meta-skills are intentionally integrated throughout.

Capacity Building · ADB

SE Asia Transport Project Preparatory — Rolling Stock Capacity Building

Six-day study tour developed for the Asian Development Bank covering Rolling Stock design and performance (MRT system), operation, communication and control, maintenance (corrective, routine, predictive), Metro/LRT depot facilities, OCC (GoA4), and manufacturing. Site visits include MRT Depot, MRT Workshop, and LRT Workshop in Singapore.

TUM · Asian Development Bank (ADB) · Singapore
Executive Education

International Immersion Program — President University MBA

One-week Singapore immersion for MBA students designed in collaboration with President University. Lectures by TUM Professors and industrial experts covering: Circular Economy in Sustainable Infrastructure, Industrial IoT for Smart Manufacturing, Data Analytics & AI, and Advanced Robotics. Industrial visits to ARTC, Yamazaki Mazak iSMART Factory, Siemens AMTC, and Hyundai HMGICS.

TUM (Munich) · President University · Singapore

Executive Education — Summer School Program Capacity Building

Intensive summer school program offering a dynamic executive education experience designed for international experts and decision-makers. Through a curated series of lectures delivered by leading TUM academics and industry pioneers, participants engage with cutting-edge theories and strategic frameworks. The curriculum is deliberately extended beyond the classroom with structured industrial visits to flagship companies and innovation hubs — including the Singapore Rail Test Centre (SRTC) and Hyundai Motor Group Innovation Center Singapore (HMGICS). Tailored for a diverse cohort blending seasoned professionals with high-potential graduate students, this program fosters a powerful network of future-oriented leaders in leadership, sustainability, and digital transformation.
